About me

22-year-old creator based in Aarhus

I'm Ellen, a UGC and iPhone content creator fluent in both Danish and English. I create aesthetic, authentic content that feels natural and relatable, while still capturing attention and reflecting each brand's identity.

I'm detail-oriented and easy to work with, and I always focus on making the collaboration smooth and enjoyable from start to finish. I really value long-term relationships and I'm proud to work with brands that choose to come back again.

Services

What I offer

From a single video to a full retainer, tailored to your brand and goals. Rates available upon request.

01

UGC Video

Vertical 9:16 format. Includes research, scriptwriting, filming, and editing. 3 months of usage rights, 1 revision. Problem/solution, hooks, testimonials, lifestyle, adapted to your brief.

02

UGC Photography

Natural lifestyle photography for social media, ads, and websites. Real moments, authentic feel, your product in everyday life or a destination setting.

03

Drone Footage

Aerial footage for hotels, venues, travel destinations, real estate, and outdoor experiences. Cinematic and platform-ready.

04

Monthly Retainer

Ongoing content creation on a monthly basis. Ideal for brands that want consistent, high-quality content without starting from scratch each time.

05

Whitelisting

Content created and approved for running as paid ads directly from my account, increased authenticity, better performance, wider reach.

06

Content Bundles

Mix of video and photo content in one package. Flexible bundles built around your campaign, launch, or ongoing content needs.

Simple, from start to finish.

02
